About Concrete Curbing

Luxe Curb and Edging provides custom designed, continuous, moulded concrete that comes in a wide variety of different shapes, colours and textures. This extremely high quality product is durable enough to be installed in any outdoor setting but still maintains the chic, modern appearance our customers desire. Curbing is designed to follow the contour of any yard, is perfectly suited for both new construction and mature yards and serves as an attractive, permanent, no-maintenance alternative to conventional landscape edging.
