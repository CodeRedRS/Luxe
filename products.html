Integral Colours
Integral colouring is a dye used to infuse concrete with rich, long-lasting, fade-resistant color. Don’t see the colour you’d like? We can dye concrete to almost any colour you would like – our sales rep will have a colour chart available during your consultation.

Accent Colours
Accent colouring produces a contrast with concrete which replicates the variegated, multi-tonal appearance of natural stone.
